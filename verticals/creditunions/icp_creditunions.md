# ICP — Credit Unions

## Sub-industries in scope
- State & Federal Credit Unions
- Community Banks

## Company size
50-500 employees, midmarket segment

## Tech stack signals
P1: Core Banking Systems, CRM platforms
P2: Help Desk systems, member portals

## Top use cases
1. Loan Origination Support - SMS updates on loan status and missing documents
2. Delinquency & Collections - Soft-touch payment reminders via text
3. Member Support/FAQ - Deflecting high-volume calls with text-based support
4. Marketing & Onboarding - Automated welcome sequences and rate promotions
5. Secure Fraud Alerts - Two-way verification conversations for suspicious activity

## Qualification signals
- High call volume for routine member inquiries
- Manual loan status communication processes
- Traditional collections approaches with low response rates
- Limited digital member engagement channels

## Competitors
- Various point solutions for each use case
- Email-only communication systems
- Traditional phone-based member service
