# Persona: Practice Manager — Wellness

## Role and Scope
Practice Managers own day-to-day operations, staff coordination, and patient experience optimization across wellness clinics. They have direct buying authority for operational tools under $500/month and strong influence on larger technology decisions that impact workflow efficiency.

## Relationship to Heymarket
Primary buyer and power user. They care about ROI metrics (appointment fill rates, lead conversion), team efficiency, and compliance. Win them with operational impact stories and clear implementation paths that won't disrupt current EMR workflows.

## Day-to-Day Pressures
- Maintaining 90%+ calendar capacity despite constant reschedules and cancellations
- Converting social media leads before they go cold (typically within 15 minutes)
- Training new front desk staff on multiple systems while maintaining service quality
- Ensuring HIPAA compliance while staff use personal phones for patient communication
- Managing pre/post-treatment workflows that require multiple touchpoints
- Coordinating communication across multiple locations with inconsistent practices
- Preventing revenue loss from no-shows and last-minute cancellations

## Key Objections and Responses
- "Our EMR already has texting" — EMR texting is one-way broadcasts, not real conversations that build relationships
- "Staff might resist another platform" — Integrates with your existing workflow, reduces manual work
- "HIPAA compliance concerns" — Enterprise-grade security with healthcare-specific compliance features
- "Budget is tight right now" — ROI from reducing no-shows typically pays for itself in first month

## Language They Use
- Fill rate, capacity optimization, no-show rate
- Lead conversion, response time, patient journey
- Treatment protocols, pre-op, post-care, aftercare
- Front desk efficiency, patient coordinator, intake process
- HIPAA compliance, patient privacy, secure messaging
- Consultation bookings, high-ticket treatments, package sales
- Patient retention, lifetime value, referral generation
- EMR integration, workflow automation, staff productivity
- Multi-location coordination, centralized operations
- Social media leads, Instagram inquiries, web forms

## What Makes Them Reply to a Cold Email
- Specific metrics about appointment fill rates or lead response times
- References to their exact EMR system or tech stack
- Industry-specific use cases (post-Botox check-ins, CoolSculpting follow-up)
- Multi-location coordination challenges

**Delete triggers:**
- Generic healthcare messaging without wellness/aesthetic focus
- Pushy sales language or immediate demo requests
- No understanding of their operational complexity
- Failure to mention HIPAA or compliance considerations

## Best Heymarket Proof Points for This Persona
- Shared inbox moves patient communication off personal cell phones with full HIPAA compliance
- 2-way texting increases appointment fill rates by reducing last-minute cancellations
- Automated pre-op and aftercare sequences reduce manual staff outreach by 60%
- Instant lead response from social media prevents lead ghosting and improves conversion
- Integrates with existing EMRs while providing conversation flexibility they lack
- Multi-location coordination through centralized communication hub

## Sequence Arc
T1–T2: Appointment logistics and fill rate optimization
T3–T4: Lead response speed and social media conversion
T5–T6: Staff efficiency and HIPAA-compliant centralization
T7–T8: Multi-location coordination and operational scaling
