# ICP — Healthcare

## Sub-industries in scope
- Hospitals
- Outpatient clinics  
- Medical groups
- Urgent care centers

## Company size
500-5000 employees, $100M-$1B revenue, midmarket segment

## Tech stack signals
P1: Epic (EHR), HubSpot or Salesforce CRM
P2: Patient engagement platforms, call center software

## Top use cases
1. Patient appointment reminders (high frequency)
2. Internal staff coordination (high frequency) 
3. Billing follow-up communications (medium frequency)
4. Discharge instructions and follow-up (medium frequency)

## Qualification signals
- HubSpot or Salesforce in technology stack
- 200+ staff members
- Active job postings for patient communications roles
- Active job postings for care coordination positions
- HIPAA compliance requirements

## Competitors
- Weave — integrated with practice management, hardware-dependent
- Relatient — patient engagement focus, limited team collaboration
- Stericycle — compliance-heavy, complex implementation
- Twilio — developer-heavy, requires custom building
