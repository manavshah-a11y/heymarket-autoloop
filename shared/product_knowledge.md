## 1. Company Overview

**What they do:** Heymarket is a business texting and messaging platform that unifies SMS, email, and popular messaging apps (WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, Apple Messages for Business, Instagram, etc.) into shared inboxes for teams.

**Mission:** Empower businesses to build relationships using intuitive, secure, and personalized messaging.

**Story:** Founded by the team behind Manymoon (an online project management app). The founders were inspired by how seamless texting with brands felt and realized businesses lacked tools to message customers at scale. Heymarket has since powered 100 million+ texts.

**Investors:** Precursor, Harrison Metal, CloudApps

**Notable customers:** Dignity Health, U-Haul, Cornell University, Methodology, Budget Dumpster, Hy-Vee, KISCO, OpusVi, Keller Williams

**Positioning tagline:** "The business messaging solution" / "Efficient Business Text Messaging"

## 2. Core Product

Heymarket is an **omnichannel business messaging platform** built around the concept of the **shared inbox** — a centralized place where teams send, receive, and manage customer conversations across SMS, email, and messaging apps. It is designed for teams (not just individuals), with collaboration, compliance, and automation built in.

### Who it's for
- **Roles:** Sales teams, customer service agents, marketing, operations/dispatch, HR/recruiting, IT administrators
- **Company size:** Teams of 2 to enterprise-scale (Custom pricing tier)
- **Industries:** Healthcare, retail/eCommerce, real estate, dispatch & operations, professional services, home services, fitness, education, financial services

## 3. Feature Deep-Dive

### 3.1 Shared Inbox
The foundational feature. A shared inbox lets an entire team send and receive messages from one centralized place — across SMS, MMS, email, Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp, Apple Messages for Business, Google Business Messages, and Instagram Messaging.

**Key capabilities:**
- Any team member can read, reply, or assign messages
- Configure separate inboxes for different teams or use cases
- Message threading — full conversation history in a single thread
- Assign conversations to specific agents
- Private internal comments (@ mention teammates) visible only to the team, not the customer
- "Waiting indicator" so teams know which conversations need attention
- Typing indicators and presence indicators
- Do not disturb mode
- Snooze conversations
- Tag and close chats for organization
- Mobile + desktop access

**Business value:** Replaces fragmented personal phone texting. Every agent has full context. No customer falls through the cracks.

**Customer proof:**
- Lighthouse: 10x increase in agent capacity — _"The biggest thing about being able to text with our customers is the relationship we're able to build. Messaging is key for referrals and retention."_
- G5 Enterprises: 12% increase in productivity — _"Replying quickly to incoming queries has empowered our team to earn customer trust and build valuable relationships."_

### 3.3 Automations

**Tagline:** "Build better workflows with rich custom field automations"

**Types of triggers:**
- Time-based (e.g., 7 days before appointment)
- Event-based (e.g., purchase made, field updated, no reply in X days)

**What automations can do:**
- Send a text or email
- Add/remove contacts from lists
- Update contact custom fields
- Assign conversations
- Launch campaigns
- Trigger cross-channel follow-ups

**Real automation examples:**
- **VIP list enrollment:** Purchase > $1,000 → Add to VIP list → Send welcome email after 1-day delay
- **Shipment tracking:** Shipping Status = "Shipped" → Send tracking link via SMS
- **Birthday message:** Birthday field = Today → Send personalized discount text
- **Appointment reminders:** 7 days out (email) → 1 day out (email) → Day-of (SMS)
- **Re-engagement:** No reply in 5 days → Send check-in email

## 4. Integrations

### CRM Integrations

#### HubSpot
- Two-way sync: text contacts directly from HubSpot without leaving the platform
- Works with all HubSpot objects (Contacts, Deals, Companies, etc.)
- Syncs HubSpot Conversations so team can view texts alongside other channels
- Auto-updates Contact and Timeline records in real time
- Trigger HubSpot Workflow automations via SMS (e.g., send a text when email goes unanswered)
- Supports Apple Business Chat, Facebook Messenger, Google Business Messages, WhatsApp — all within HubSpot
- Enterprise reporting: CSAT, response times, team productivity
- SMS open rates 5x higher than email; response times as fast as 90 seconds

#### Salesforce
- Native Lightning widget — text from any Salesforce object (Contacts, Leads, Accounts, Cases, Custom Objects)
- Available on Salesforce AppExchange
- Shared inbox integrated into Sales Cloud and Marketing Cloud
- Texts auto-log to Heymarket Message Object and link to Salesforce records
- Use Salesforce data as merge tokens for personalized mass texts
- Salesforce Flows integration for automated text triggers
- Marketing Cloud Journey Builder integration for SMS in drip sequences
- Built-in Salesforce SMS reports — no setup required
- Available as an Add-on ($7.50/user/month) on all plans

## 9. Competitive Differentiators

**vs. one-way SMS broadcast tools (e.g., SimpleTexting, Attentive):**
- Two-way conversations, not just blasts
- Team collaboration built in (shared inbox, assignments, private comments)
- Full conversation history and contact context

**vs. Zipwhip (sunset Dec 2023 — Twilio recommended Heymarket as preferred replacement):**
- Unlimited group message sizes (Zipwhip capped at 100)
- Shared inboxes and private comments (Zipwhip had none)
- Advanced automation triggers (Zipwhip: keyword-only)
- Campaign sequences with engagement tracking
- Full integration ecosystem (HubSpot, Teams, Shopify, Slack, Zendesk)
- Omnichannel (Zipwhip was SMS-only)
- SAML SSO, MFA, HIPAA, CCPA/CPRA (Zipwhip: limited security)
- Smooth migration: phone numbers, contacts, opt-in lists, templates, message history all transfer

**vs. personal phone texting:**
- Shared visibility — no customer conversations lost when an agent leaves
- Compliance and opt-out management built in
- Reporting and audit trail
- Scale: templates, automations, broadcasts

**Core differentiator framing:**
> Heymarket is for teams. It's not a single-rep SMS tool — it's a collaborative, enterprise-grade messaging platform with the compliance, integrations, and AI tools that make it work at scale.
